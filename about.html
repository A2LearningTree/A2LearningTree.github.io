About

My name is Abhijay Deevi. I am an 11th grader in Del Norte High School. Throughout my life, I took many classes with tutors, especially for English. I spent lots of money on some tutors and tried to get better. The only problem was that I wasn't getting better at English and I was just burning money.

I tried looking for tutors, but I could barely find any. It took me a while to even find a single tutor. After a while, I was finally able to find one that helped me improve my english. It took me a lot of money and time to find a tutor that followed my pace and taught me what I need to know.

This got me thinking because I wanted to make finding the right tutor easier for other people. That is how I landed on this idea where the customer would be able to look through our members and choose which tutor they liked and switch if they felt like they needed someone else.

I knew I could start this company up because I had previous experience with my club, Coding4Kidz. In Coding4Kidz, I managed many members and taught over 200 students with my club members. Because of this experience, I feel like I have the necessary requirements to start a company and make sure it becomes really good.

Hopefully this service helps you out and gives you a great learning experience!

Abhijay Deevi

Founder & CEO
