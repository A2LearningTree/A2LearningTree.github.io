A2LearningTree

Everyone knows it's very hard to find a tutor that suits a student's specific needs. Every student learns at a different pace, so it's hard to find a tutor that teaches at the same speed. In addition to this, all schools teach their subject differently, so it's hard for the tutor to figure out what they need to teach.

This is where A2LearningTree comes in. The first benefit is that you can always choose the tutor and schedule a class based on your own schedule. Second, the tutors all understand exactly what they need to teach, since they all took that exact class in the past. This allows the tutor to teach at the correct pace, which helps the student understand the subject better. There are numerous high level tutors that teach various different subjects, allowing students to learn and understand anything that they choose.

Classes are an hour long and can be taken as many times a day as you want.

  1. High schoolers know exactly what to teach
  2. Choose what dates you want
  3. Very affordable
  4. Tutoring sessions are completely online
  5. First class is free!
