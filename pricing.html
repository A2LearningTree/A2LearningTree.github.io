Pricing

The price of the class is very affordable. Regular tutoring costs more than 50 dollars and even goes up to 200 to 300 dollars! Many tutors I have seen charge up to 200 dollars for an SAT class that only lasts for an hour. Our prices are MUCH cheaper than that.

A2LearningTree charges 30 dollars for a 1 hour class. This applies to each and every class that we give (including SAT and other harder classes like that).

The first class is always free.

Problems

There might be a time when a tutor is not teaching well and multiple students are not satisfied with there teaching. We try to stop this from happening by reviewing how tutors are doing and correcting them on the way. However, if this still happens then we will gladly give you free classes based on the number of class you took with the bad tutor. The rest of our tutors are usually good and most people like how they are teaching.
